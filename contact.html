Parking Directions

The Visitor Welcome Center and Bookstore parking garage is located at the Reitz Union, at the corner of Museum Road and Reitz Union Drive. The garage is an unrestricted pay facility available to all members of the university community.

Standard daily fee:

$5.00 (159 spaces available)

Hours of operation:

Monday - Friday: 7:30 am - 4:30 pm*

The garage may be used during non-operating hours for short-term parking, free of charge.

* Short-term and daily fees apply during this time. They are to be paid at pay stations located at the stair towers and elevators in the garage.
